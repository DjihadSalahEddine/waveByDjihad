A Time Chaine organizes events linked to each other in certain flow. So that an event won't happen without the effect of the previous one.
Following our Link of events represented in the cards bellow will help you understanding how the wireless key works.

Starting from The Pin Lock which I bet you deal with more than once daily.

And the mechanism inside that tool will actually lead to a huge series of inventions.

Or maybe just the use of it in past helped keeping knowledge and tools safe so they can use to invent some devises.

1

the pin lock

The Pin Lock is a tool used for security and privacy, and it's a mechanism for keeping any container closed. It opens using its own key that has the pin pattern. It was first created by ancient Egyptians 6000 years ago.

More

The Pin Lock has been used on a global scale since it was invented. As it inspired Greeks in the 3rd centry B.C to create Gears.

The invention of Gears made a huge revolution in industry through the years in construction and trasportation.

In the early years of the 19th century, a German inventor thought about creating a machine to transport one person due to a smart design of gears. That's how the world's first bicycle was made.

2

the bicycle

A transportation machine for individuals made with a sat of gears to transform the energy generated by the human body (muscles) into kinetic energy. It was first made in 1817 by Karl Von Drais.

More

A few decades after making the world's first bicycle, inventor starts thinking of using another source of energy rather than the human energy and the animal energy (horses...) and instead of steam powered engine.

Using a certain set of gears, mechanical tools and some chemistry, an American engeneer made the first car engine.

3

the car engine

The Car Engine is a machine that converts the energy from the form of Heat made as a result of gas combustion into mechanical energy. The world's first gas engine was made in 1886 by Carl Benz.

More

Cars has developed through the years untill nowadays. Pin locks were used to secure them for a long time, untill The Wireless Key was made.

It was made as an application for what we call: Radio Waves.

4

the wireless key

The Wireless Key is a device that uses Radio Waves to send the locking or the unlocking pin to the radio receiver inside the car causing it to lock or unlock the doors.
