background

Security and privacy have been very sensitive to us. So we used the tech we have to protect them. Starting from a simple pin lock to some very complicated locks and cybersecurity protocols including "THE WAVE".



Even thought you can't see them, but Waves have been taking place in our daily life. Phones calls, internet connections, TVs, Radios, telecommunication and security.



Binary information is sent as a form of waves. The transmitter gathers and decodes information and the receiver displays the information in an audible and/or visual form that consumers can view or listen to.



mechanism

To understand how a car wireless key works, you need to know how radio waves work (as shown above).


When you press the open or close button on your key, a pin code is sent in binary as a Radio wave. The device on the car receives the code and compare it with the one stored in it. When the cades matche, the doors open or close depends on the button you pressed.



To avoid hackers' listenting attacks, modern car security devices use a new method of generating a new code everytime you open or close your doors using the wireless key.
The code algorithm is saved inside the key and inside the car.
